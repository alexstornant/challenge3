Current Experiments going on at SpaceY

Interstellar Dust Composition Analysis

To analyze the composition of interstellar dust particles to better understand the chemical makeup of the universe and its potential implications for planetary formation.

Solar Wind Interaction with Magnetospheres

To investigate the interaction between solar wind and planetary magnetospheres to better comprehend space weather phenomena and its impact on space missions and terrestrial infrastructure.

Exoplanet Atmosphere Characterization

To analyze the atmospheres of distant exoplanets using spectroscopic techniques to identify potential signs of habitability and biosignatures
